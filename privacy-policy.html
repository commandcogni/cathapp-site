Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 30, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to CathApp. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and use.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. CathApp is designed with your privacy in mind and operates without gathering, storing, or processing any user data.

No Data Collection

CathApp does not:

  • Collect personal information (such as name, email, or phone number)
  • Track your location
  • Access your contacts, photos, or other device data
  • Use cookies or similar tracking technologies
  • Collect usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Share any information with third parties

No Third-Party Services

CathApp does not integrate with any third-party services that would collect your data. There are no advertisements, analytics platforms, or external tracking mechanisms within the app.

Children's Privacy

Since CathApp does not collect any data, it is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13.
